# Guaraní Currency Converter

**[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6760961975)** · **[Website](https://alexryakhin.github.io/guarani-currency-converter-page/)**

A calm, focused **iPhone and iPad** app for converting **Paraguayan guaraní (PYG)** against the currencies people use every day. The layout is easy to read, the math stays upfront, and your conversion history stays **on your device**.

## What you can do

- **Convert as you type** between two currencies and see the implied rate when it is available.
- **Swap** currencies in one tap. When you already have a result, the amount can carry over so you do not have to retype.
- **Browse history** of past conversions on this device, delete single rows, or clear everything when you want a fresh start.
- **Rely on live rates** when you are online, with sensible fallbacks when the network is slow or unavailable so you can still get a figure.

## Who it is for

Anyone who thinks in **PYG** but also in **USD**, **EUR**, **BRL**, **ARS**, or other major currencies: locals, travelers, expats, and people sending or planning money across borders.

## A quick note on rates

The app is a **utility**, not financial advice. Banks, cards, and transfer services use their own rates and fees. Always double-check before you commit to a real transaction.
